Downtown Santa Monica Farmer's Market

Explore Downtown Santa Monica Farmer's Market

April-June, 2023

Join us for an exciting day of fresh produce, artisanal foods, live music, and more at the Explore Downtown Santa Monica Farmer's Market! Every Wednesday from 8:00 am to 1:00 pm, the Third Street Promenade transforms into a vibrant marketplace, offering a diverse selection of locally grown fruits and vegetables, handcrafted goods, and unique culinary experiences.

Stroll through the market and discover a variety of fresh, seasonal produce from local farmers, including organic and heirloom options. Sample delicious artisanal foods, such as artisanal bread, cheeses, olives, and pickles, from the market's many food vendors. And don't forget to grab a fresh-squeezed juice or smoothie made with locally sourced ingredients.

Downtown Santa Monica Farmer's Market Live Music Show & Workshops

April-June, 2023

In addition to the food and beverage offerings, the market also features live music performances from local musicians and artists, adding to the festive atmosphere. And for those who want to learn more about sustainable food practices, the market offers educational workshops and demonstrations on topics such as composting, beekeeping, and cooking with seasonal produce.

Located in the heart of Downtown Santa Monica, the Explore Downtown Santa Monica Farmer's Market is the perfect destination for a mid-week break, a family outing, or a fun date. So come join us, support local agriculture and sustainable food practices, and discover the best of what Santa Monica has to offer!
