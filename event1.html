Underwood Family Farms Pick Your Own Tomatoes

Tomato Picking Event Week 1

May 17, 2023

Pick-Your-Own Romas are 40¢ per pound. Already picked Romas are 70¢ per pound & must be ordered & pre-paid. Pre-paid orders begin April 10. Cases of bell peppers will also be available for purchase. In addition, fresh basil will be available at checkout for $9.00 per 1 lb bag. To order, please call our Moorpark Farm at (805) 529-3690.

If bringing your own containers, they must be of equal size that will not hold over 50 lbs. to make it easier for weighing. Containers must be placed on the scale by the harvester upon entry and at check-out.If they are all the same, we will only need to weigh one of two. If they are dissimilar, we'll have to weigh each one and the weight of the containers will be noted and deducted from the final weight. Boxes will be available for $2.00/box.

Tomato Picking Event Week 2 & Tomato Sauce Competition

Title description, Sep 2, 2017

Welcome to the annual Tomato Sauce Competition, where the best chefs and home cooks from all around gather to showcase their tomato sauce creations and compete for the coveted title of "Best Tomato Sauce"!

This year's competition will be held at the picturesque Town Square on Saturday, May 7th, from 10am to 5pm. We invite all tomato sauce enthusiasts to join us for a day of delicious food, live music, and friendly competition.Contestants will have the chance to present their tomato sauce to a panel of judges made up of local food critics, chefs, and tomato sauce experts. The judges will evaluate each sauce based on taste, texture, aroma, and overall presentation.
