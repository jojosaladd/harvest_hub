Somis Family Picnic

June 10th, 2023

Come join us for a day of fun and furry friends at the Somis Animal Center Family Picnic! On Saturday, June 25th from 11:00 am to 3:00 pm, bring your family and friends to enjoy a picnic lunch, live music, games, and activities with the cutest animals in town.

Located in the scenic countryside of Somis, California, the Somis Animal Center is home to a variety of rescued animals, including horses, goats, pigs, chickens, and more. During the family picnic, visitors will have the opportunity to meet these friendly animals up close, and learn about the center's mission to provide a safe and loving home for these creatures in need.
